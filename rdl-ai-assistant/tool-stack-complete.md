# RDL AI Employees - Complete Tool Stack & Pricing

## Voice AI Services (Phone Calls)

### Option 1: Vapi.ai (Recommended for flexibility)
- **Pricing:** $0.05/min + Twilio telephony fees (~$0.013/min)
- **Phone numbers:** $10/month per line
- **Concurrency:** 10 concurrent calls for $10/month
- **Best for:** Developers building custom workflows
- **Pros:** Modular, excellent integrations, transparent pricing
- **Cons:** Requires some setup/development work
- **Estimated cost for 1 client (500 min/mo):** ~$32/mo

### Option 2: Retell AI (Best for ease of use)
- **Pricing:** $0.07-$0.08/min (conversation engine) + telephony
- **Phone numbers:** Included in some plans
- **Best for:** Quick deployment, no-code setup
- **Pros:** Great voice quality, simple dashboard, fast setup
- **Cons:** Slightly higher per-minute cost
- **Estimated cost for 1 client (500 min/mo):** ~$40-$45/mo

### Option 3: Bland AI (Enterprise-focused)
- **Pricing:** $0.09/min outbound, $0.04/min inbound
- **Phone numbers:** $15/month per number
- **Best for:** High-volume call centers
- **Pros:** Enterprise-grade, good for outbound sales calls
- **Cons:** Higher minimums, more complex pricing
- **Estimated cost for 1 client (500 min/mo):** ~$45-$50/mo

### Option 4: Goodcall (All-in-one virtual receptionist)
- **Pricing:** Starts at ~$299/month (variable, check current)
- **Phone numbers:** Included
- **Best for:** Small businesses wanting turnkey solution
- **Pros:** Fully managed, no setup required
- **Cons:** Highest monthly minimum, less customization
- **Estimated cost for 1 client:** $299+/mo
